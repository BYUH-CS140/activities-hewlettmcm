“Aloha: Life of an International Student at BYU Hawaii”

Another beautiful day in paradise. I’m Hewlett Madriaga, and I’m from the Philippines. I never imagined I’d be living and studying at BYU–Hawaii!

BYU–Hawaii isn’t just a school—it’s a multicultural hub. We have students from over 70 countries. Every day feels like the world is just next door.

Most students work part-time. I work at the Polynesian Cultural Center as a content creator for the Marketing Department. It’s more than just a job; it’s a way to share culture and earn my way through school.

Being far from home is hard, but my ohana here makes me feel like I’m never alone.

Life as an international student at BYU–Hawaii is full of learning, growing, and aloha. I’m not just getting a degree but also I’m building a global family.
